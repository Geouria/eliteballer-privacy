Privacy Policy

App: Elite Baller  ·  Developer: Filip Lindkvist

Effective date: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Elite Baller ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Elite Baller mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Guest mode: If you choose to use the App without creating an account ("Try without an account"), we do not collect any personal data. This policy applies only to registered users.

1. Information We Collect

When you register and use the App, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: email address and display name provided during registration.
  • Training data: drill names, training session timestamps, and session durations that you log in the App.
  • Forum activity: playlists you share, comments you post, and votes you cast in the community forum.
  • Device and usage data: basic technical information (such as device type and OS version) collected automatically by our third-party services for analytics and ad delivery purposes.

We do not collect your location, phone number, payment information, or any sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your user account.
  • Provide the core App features, including drill tracking, history, and the community forum.
  • Send account-related emails (e.g. email verification and password resets).
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • Improve the App's performance and fix issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with its own privacy practices:

Firebase Authentication (Google LLC)
Used to securely manage user accounts and email verification. Firebase may process your email address to authenticate your identity. See Firebase Privacy and Security.

Cloud Firestore (Google LLC)
Your training data, display name, and forum content are stored in Firestore, a cloud database operated by Google. Data is stored on Google's servers. See Google Privacy Policy.

Google AdMob (Google LLC)
The App displays banner and interstitial advertisements served by AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalised or contextual ads. You can review and adjust your ad preferences via your device settings (Ads > Reset Advertising ID / Opt out of ads personalisation). See AdMob Privacy.

Beyond the Google services listed above, we do not share your personal data with any other third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google's Firebase / Cloud Firestore infrastructure, which employs industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. We apply appropriate security rules to restrict access to your data so that only you and authorised App functions can read or modify it.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your account data for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide the App. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed in accordance with the deletion process described in Section 7.

5. Children's Privacy

Elite Baller is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at noreply@eliteballer.app and we will promptly delete that information.

For users between 13 and 16 in the European Economic Area, parental or guardian consent may be required in accordance with applicable national law.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have the following rights regarding your personal data under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can request that inaccurate or incomplete data be corrected.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): You can request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You can object to certain types of processing, including direct marketing.
  • Right to restrict processing: You can request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at noreply@eliteballer.app. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. Account and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time by sending an email to noreply@eliteballer.app with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and the email address associated with your account.

Upon receiving your request, we will delete your account and personal data (including training history and forum content) within a reasonable timeframe. Note that some data may be retained for a short period in backups before being permanently removed.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we may notify you via the App or by email. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. The current version is always available at eliteballer.app.
